what is drxnk.co?

drxnk.co is a libertarian cooperative committed to demonstrations of our freedom of choice. this is typically done via unashamed inebriation.

how do i join?

to preserve anonymity, membership is available only in the lead up to and during demonstrations. this temporary membership requires a nominal fee and will expire at conclusion of any demonstration.

you will find this link active if a demonstration is planned in the near future.

when you say demonstration, you mean..?

oh yes, we mean getting hammered.

how do i know when a "demonstration" will be held?

for anonymity, we don't want your details on record. tbc how we'll provide public updates.

what else do i need to know?

we may ask you to split the cost of a keg or two. it's worth it.
